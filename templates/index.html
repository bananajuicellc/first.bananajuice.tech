Click the arrow to find out who goes first.

Have you played a board game but didn't like the recommended way to choose the first player? Is your gaming group bored with throwing dice to choose? Who Goes First is a new way to choose the first player.

Open it and get a random card. It could be interactive or have your friends tell the best story. Use it to pick the next player, or if you don't like it: tap the arrow to get a new card.

We are working on an app version for iOS and Android! Please subscribe to our mailing list to be the first to find out when it is ready.

Who Goes First is a project of Timothy and Alexandra Swast and our friends, born out of our love for board games. {% endblock %}
